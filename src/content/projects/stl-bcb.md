The St. Louis BCB Collective serves as a hub for the bioinformatics and computational biology community in the St. Louis metropolitan area. The platform connects researchers, developers, and scientists working at the intersection of biology and computation.

Built to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing across institutions including SLU, WashU, and the broader research ecosystem.
